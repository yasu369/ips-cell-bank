FAQ

Plain answers for serious clients.

Does iPS cell banking guarantee future treatment?

No. Banking preserves a cellular resource and future optionality. Any future treatment depends on medical evidence, regulatory approval, physician judgment, and the client's condition at that time.

Can Double-K say this is a Kyoto University service?

No. The proper expression is that the program uses Kyoto University licensed iPS cell technology, within the scope of the license agreement. It should not imply endorsement unless separately authorized.

Who is this for?

International clients who want a long-term, professionally governed cell banking relationship in Japan and who understand that the service is about preparedness, not guaranteed intervention.
